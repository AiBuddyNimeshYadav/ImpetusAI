# Leave Policy — Impetus Technologies

## 1. Annual Leave (Earned Leave)
- All full-time employees are entitled to **24 days** of annual leave per calendar year.
- Annual leave is credited at the rate of **2 days per month** of completed service.
- Leave can be carried forward to the next year, up to a maximum of **10 days**.
- Any leave beyond the carry-forward limit will lapse at the end of the calendar year.
- Employees must apply for annual leave at least **3 working days** in advance.
- For leave exceeding 5 consecutive days, approval from the reporting manager **and** HR is required.

## 2. Sick Leave
- Employees are entitled to **12 days** of sick leave per year.
- Sick leave beyond **3 consecutive days** requires a medical certificate from a registered practitioner.
- Sick leave cannot be carried forward and lapses at the end of the calendar year.
- In case of extended illness (more than 10 days), employees may apply for medical leave under the company's extended illness policy.

## 3. Casual Leave
- Employees are entitled to **6 days** of casual leave per year.
- Casual leave cannot be taken for more than **3 consecutive days** at a time.
- These must be applied for at least **1 day in advance** except in emergencies.
- Casual leave cannot be carried forward.

## 4. Maternity Leave
- Female employees are entitled to **26 weeks** of paid maternity leave for the first two children.
- For the third child onwards, maternity leave is **12 weeks**.
- Maternity leave can commence up to **8 weeks** before the expected delivery date.
- Employees must notify HR at least **2 months** before the anticipated leave start date.

## 5. Paternity Leave
- Male employees are entitled to **10 working days** of paternity leave.
- Paternity leave must be taken within **3 months** of the child's birth.
- Only **1 instance** of paternity leave per child is allowed.

## 6. Bereavement Leave
- Employees are entitled to **5 days** of bereavement leave in case of the death of an immediate family member (spouse, parent, child, sibling).
- For extended family members, **2 days** of bereavement leave is provided.

## 7. Public Holidays
- Impetus Technologies observes **12 public holidays** per year as per the holiday calendar published annually by HR.
- The list includes national holidays and a selection of regional/festival holidays.
- In case an employee works on a public holiday, they are entitled to a compensatory off within the same month.

## 8. Leave Without Pay (LWP)
- If all leave quotas are exhausted, employees may apply for leave without pay.
- LWP requires approval from the Department Head and HR Director.
- Extended LWP beyond **15 days** may affect benefits eligibility and performance evaluation.

## 9. Compensatory Off
- Employees required to work on weekends or public holidays are entitled to compensatory time off.
- Compensatory off must be availed within **30 days** of the working day.
- A maximum of **2 compensatory offs** can be accumulated at any time.

## 10. Leave Application Process
1. Submit leave request via the HR portal or ImpetusAI platform.
2. The request is routed to the reporting manager for approval.
3. Upon manager approval, HR is notified for records.
4. Employees receive a confirmation notification via email.
5. For emergency leave, employees may apply retroactively within **2 working days** of return.
