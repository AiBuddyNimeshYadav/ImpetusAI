# Code of Conduct — Impetus Technologies

## 1. Purpose
This Code of Conduct establishes the standards of professional behavior expected of all employees at Impetus Technologies. It applies to all full-time, part-time, contract, and temporary employees across all locations.

## 2. Professional Behavior
- Treat all colleagues, clients, and stakeholders with **respect and dignity**.
- Maintain a **harassment-free workplace**. Discrimination based on race, gender, religion, age, disability, sexual orientation, or national origin is strictly prohibited.
- Use professional and courteous language in all communications (email, chat, meetings).
- Avoid gossip, rumor-spreading, or any behavior that undermines team morale.

## 3. Workplace Ethics
- Act with **integrity and honesty** in all business dealings.
- Avoid **conflicts of interest**. Disclose any relationships or investments that may conflict with company interests.
- Protect **confidential information** including client data, trade secrets, and proprietary technology.
- Do not use company resources for personal gain or unauthorized purposes.
- Report any suspected fraud, corruption, or unethical behavior to the Ethics Hotline or your manager.

## 4. Attendance and Punctuality
- Employees are expected to maintain regular attendance and be punctual.
- Core working hours are **9:30 AM to 6:30 PM** (with flexibility per project/team).
- Inform your manager and update the attendance system if you are going to be late or absent.
- Repeated unexplained absences may result in disciplinary action.

## 5. Dress Code
- Business casual attire is expected in office settings.
- On client-facing days, **business formal** attire is required.
- Casual Fridays are observed — jeans and polo shirts are acceptable.
- Work-from-home employees should dress appropriately for video meetings.

## 6. Use of Company Resources
- Company laptops, phones, and equipment are provided for professional use.
- Limited personal use is acceptable but should not interfere with work.
- Do not install unauthorized software on company devices.
- All data stored on company devices is subject to audit.

## 7. Communication Guidelines
- Use official channels (company email, Slack, Teams) for work communication.
- Personal social media use during work hours should be minimal.
- Do not share confidential or proprietary information on social media.
- Represent the company professionally in all external communications.

## 8. Anti-Harassment Policy
- Impetus Technologies has a **zero-tolerance policy** for workplace harassment.
- Harassment includes: unwanted physical contact, verbal abuse, intimidation, cyberbullying, and sexual harassment.
- Any employee who experiences or witnesses harassment should report it to HR immediately.
- Reports can be made confidentially and without fear of retaliation.
- Investigations will be conducted promptly and fairly.

## 9. Substance Abuse
- The workplace is a **drug-free environment**. Use of illegal substances is strictly prohibited.
- Alcohol consumption is not permitted during work hours or on company premises, except at company-approved events.
- Employees suspected of substance abuse may be asked to undergo testing.

## 10. Disciplinary Action
Violations of this Code of Conduct may result in disciplinary action, which may include:
1. Verbal warning
2. Written warning
3. Suspension without pay
4. Termination of employment

The severity of the action will depend on the nature and frequency of the violation.
