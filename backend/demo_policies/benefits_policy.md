# Employee Benefits Policy — Impetus Technologies

## 1. Health Insurance
### 1.1 Group Medical Insurance
- All full-time employees are covered under the **Group Medical Insurance** plan from **Day 1** of joining.
- Coverage: **₹5,00,000** (Five Lakhs) per annum for employees at Band C and below.
- Coverage: **₹10,00,000** (Ten Lakhs) per annum for employees at Band D and above.
- The policy covers: hospitalization, day-care procedures, pre/post-hospitalization expenses (30/60 days), ambulance charges.
- **Dependents covered**: Spouse, up to 2 children, and parents (parents coverage available with employee co-pay).
- Pre-existing diseases are covered after a **waiting period of 2 years**.

### 1.2 Dental and Vision
- Basic dental checkups are covered under the health insurance plan (up to ₹10,000/year).
- Vision benefits include one eye examination per year and spectacle reimbursement up to ₹5,000/year.

### 1.3 Term Life Insurance
- All employees are covered under a **Group Term Life Insurance** policy.
- Sum assured: **3x annual CTC** (Cost to Company).
- Accidental death and disability coverage: **5x annual CTC**.

## 2. Retirement Benefits
### 2.1 Provident Fund (PF)
- Employee contribution: **12% of Basic Salary** (mandatory).
- Employer contribution: **12% of Basic Salary** (statutory).
- Employees can opt for higher voluntary contributions (VPF) up to 100% of Basic Salary.

### 2.2 Gratuity
- Employees who complete **5 years** of continuous service are eligible for gratuity.
- Calculation: (15/26) × Last drawn Basic Salary × Years of Completed Service.
- Maximum gratuity payable: ₹20,00,000 as per the Payment of Gratuity Act.

### 2.3 National Pension Scheme (NPS)
- Employees can opt to contribute to NPS for additional retirement savings.
- The company does not match NPS contributions but facilitates payroll deductions.
- Tax benefits under Section 80CCD are applicable for NPS contributions.

## 3. Reimbursements
### 3.1 Travel Reimbursement
- Local travel for official purposes is reimbursable upon submission of receipts.
- Auto/cab fare or public transport (bus, metro) expenses are covered.
- Two-wheeler fuel reimbursement: ₹5/km (with log maintained).
- For inter-city travel, refer to the Travel Policy document.

### 3.2 Mobile Phone Reimbursement
- Employees in Band D and above receive a monthly mobile reimbursement of **₹1,500**.
- Employees in Band C receive **₹750/month**.
- Reimbursement is processed monthly through payroll upon submission of bills.

### 3.3 Learning and Development
- Each employee has an annual **learning budget of ₹30,000**.
- This can be used for: online courses (Udemy, Coursera), certifications (AWS, GCP, Azure), conferences, workshops.
- Certification exam fees are **100% reimbursable** upon passing.
- Conference attendance requires manager approval and is covered up to ₹50,000/year (including travel).

### 3.4 Meal Allowance
- Employees working from office receive a daily meal subsidy of **₹200/day** via the company cafeteria or Sodexo card.
- Remote employees do not receive meal allowance.

## 4. Wellness Benefits
### 4.1 Gym/Fitness Reimbursement
- Annual fitness reimbursement of up to **₹12,000** for gym memberships, yoga classes, or sports activities.
- Receipts/invoices must be submitted quarterly.

### 4.2 Employee Assistance Program (EAP)
- Free and confidential counseling services available for all employees and their families.
- Covers: stress management, relationship issues, financial counseling, legal guidance.
- Access via: eap@impetus.com or the dedicated helpline.

### 4.3 Annual Health Checkup
- The company organizes a **free annual health checkup** for all employees.
- Typically conducted in Q1 of each calendar year at the office premises or partner hospitals.

## 5. Other Benefits
### 5.1 Referral Bonus
- Employee referral bonus: **₹25,000 to ₹1,00,000** depending on the role and level.
- Referral bonus is paid after the referred employee completes **3 months** of service.

### 5.2 Long Service Awards
- 3 years: Certificate of Appreciation + ₹5,000 gift voucher.
- 5 years: Plaque + ₹15,000 gift voucher + 1 extra day of leave.
- 10 years: Crystal trophy + ₹30,000 gift voucher + 2 extra days of leave + featured on internal newsletter.

### 5.3 Relocation Assistance
- Employees relocating for work receive a one-time relocation allowance.
- Within India: up to **₹50,000** for moving expenses.
- International relocation follows a separate international assignment policy.

## 6. Benefits Enrollment
- New employees are auto-enrolled in health insurance and PF on joining.
- Changes to beneficiaries or dependent coverage can be made during the **annual open enrollment period** (January 1–15 each year).
- Contact HR at benefits@impetus.com for queries about benefits enrollment.
